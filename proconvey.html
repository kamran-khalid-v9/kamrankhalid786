Primary Technical Architect for a £202,929 Innovate UK Smart Grant award-winning PropTech platform.

The Mission: Disrupting the UK’s outdated residential conveyancing market—a sector where manual processes cost homebuyers £607 million annually.

The Outcome: Scaled from concept to a production-ready system supporting 2,200+ clients, achieving an 80% reduction in legal inquiries through automated "Exchange-Ready" packs.

Leadership & Main Features

As Senior Software Developer, I was the primary architect for the backend lifecycle that satisfied Innovate UK Smart Grant milestones. My leadership focused on backend architecture, compliance automation, and grant-critical delivery. I engineered AI-driven KYC/KYB decisioning and automated Companies House & property data retrieval. Mission-critical integrations included Yoti, Stripe, Sprift, Royal Mail AddressNow, and Companies House. The main feature I developed was the automated “exchange-ready” document pack (TA6/TA7/TA10), which cut onboarding from weeks to hours and reduced legal inquiries by 80%. Architectural refactors reduced technical debt and enabled scale to 2,200+ clients.
